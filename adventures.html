About Me

Hi! My name is Owen Jensen. My name is Owen Jensen, and I am currently a student at Brigham Young University pursuing a degree in accounting. I have a strong passion for numbers and problem-solving, which has driven me to excel in my studies and seek out opportunities in the field of accounting.

I'm a very principle-based person. I identify important principle in my life and live by them. This is one of my driving principles: Life is only as good as you make it! We must have hobbies and spend time doing the things that bring us joy. Here are some of my hobbies:

  1. Sports

      • College Football & Basketball (BYU Cougars!)

      • National Basketball Association (Sacramento Kings)
      • The Major Golf Tournaments (Masters, PGA Championship, U.S. Open, British Open)
    • I also enjoy playing sports, especially basketball and football
  2. Traveling

    • In my adventures so far, I have:

      • Traveled to 6 different countries including Canada, Germany, Italy, Switzerland, France, and England
      • Seen many historial sites including the Colosseum, Vatican City, the Tower Bridge, and the Eiffel Tower
  3. Reading
    • I love reading short articles about sports or news. Some of my favorite publications are:
      • The Athletic

      • The Wall Street Journal
    • I enjoy reading both fiction and non-fiction books. Some of my favorite books are:
      • "Fight Club" by Chuck Palahniuk
      • "Steve Jobs" by Walter Isaacson
